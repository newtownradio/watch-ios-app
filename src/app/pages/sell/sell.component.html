📋 Legal Terms & Sale Process

🛒 Seller Terms & Responsibilities

Before Listing:

  • Authentication Required: All watches must be verified by our trusted third-party partners
  • Government ID: Valid government ID upload is mandatory for legal compliance
  • Ownership Verification: You must legally own the watch you're selling
  • Accurate Description: Provide detailed, honest condition and history information

During Sale:

  • 3-Month Window: Your listing is active for 3 months from creation date
  • Bid Management: You can accept, decline, or make counteroffers (max 3)
  • Communication: Respond to buyer inquiries within 24 hours
  • Price Negotiation: Counteroffers must be reasonable and market-appropriate

After Sale:

  • Secure Shipping: Ship authenticated watch within 3 business days
  • Insurance: Insure high-value items during transit
  • Documentation: Provide tracking and authentication paperwork
  • Funds Release: Funds held in escrow until buyer confirms receipt

🛍️ Buyer Terms & Protections

Before Purchase:

  • Pre-Authenticated: All watches are verified by third-party experts
  • Full Documentation: Access to authentication reports and condition details
  • Seller Verification: View seller ratings and transaction history
  • Market Research: Compare prices and verify authenticity independently

During Purchase:

  • Bidding Process: Place bids or make direct offers on listings
  • Counteroffer Rights: Accept, decline, or counter seller offers
  • Payment Security: Funds held securely in platform escrow
  • Clear Terms: All sale terms and conditions clearly stated

After Purchase:

  • 72-Hour Review: Inspect received watch and documentation
  • Return Rights: Return policy applies if item doesn't match description
  • Ownership Transfer: Receive full ownership documentation
  • Dispute Resolution: Platform support for any issues or concerns

🔒 Verification & Authentication Process

Third-Party Partners:

  • Expert Network: Professional watchmakers and authentication specialists
  • Multiple Methods: Physical inspection, movement analysis, documentation review
  • Quality Standards: All partners meet our reliability and accuracy requirements
  • Ongoing Monitoring: Regular performance reviews and quality assurance

Authentication Requirements:

  • Physical Examination: Detailed inspection of watch components
  • Movement Analysis: Verification of internal mechanisms and authenticity
  • Documentation Review: Analysis of papers, receipts, and service records
  • Condition Assessment: Comprehensive evaluation of wear and modifications

Post-Authentication:

  • Detailed Reports: Comprehensive authentication documentation
  • Condition Photos: High-quality images of verified condition
  • Market Valuation: Professional assessment of current market value
  • Legal Compliance: Verification of lawful ownership and acquisition
